Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about selling your diabetic test strips

We Buy Test Strips Oklahoma is a company that purchases unused, unexpired diabetic test strips from individuals who have excess supplies. We then make these supplies available to others who need them at affordable prices.

Selling your unused test strips helps others who need them but can't afford retail prices. It also prevents waste of perfectly good medical supplies and gives you cash for items you don't need.

Yes, it is legal to sell test strips that you purchased. If you received them through Medicare or Medicaid, however, you cannot legally sell them. All boxes must be unopened and unexpired.

Simply visit our "Sell" page, select the brand and quantity of test strips you have, and follow the checkout process. We'll provide a prepaid shipping label, and once we receive and verify your items, we'll send payment.

Test strips must be unopened, unexpired, and in their original sealed packaging. Boxes should not be damaged, and the expiration date should be at least 6 months away.

We accept most major brands including Accu-Chek, OneTouch, Contour, FreeStyle, and more. Visit our "Sell" page to see the complete list of accepted brands and current prices.

We offer payment via PayPal, Venmo, or check. You can select your preferred payment method during checkout. Payments are typically processed within 1-2 business days after we receive and verify your items.

Payment amounts vary based on brand, quantity, and expiration date. Current prices are listed on our "Sell" page. Generally, boxes with longer expiration dates and more popular brands command higher prices.
