Terms & Conditions

Please read these terms carefully before selling your supplies

Effective Date: May 17, 2025

Please read the following Terms and Conditions carefully before sending your diabetic supplies to We Buy Test Strips Oklahoma (WBTSOK). By completing a sell order on our website, you acknowledge and agree to these terms.

WBTSOK does not return any items that do not meet our eligibility requirements unless otherwise stated below. Be sure all supplies fall within our guidelines prior to shipping. If products meet all terms and conditions, WBTSOK agrees to issue payment within 24 hours of processing your package.

1. Age and Ownership

  • You must be at least 18 years old.
  • You must be the rightful owner of the items you are selling, or legally authorized to sell on behalf of the rightful owner.
  • By sending us your supplies, you transfer legal ownership of those items to WBTSOK.

2. Product Condition

  • We only accept factory-sealed, unexpired, unopened boxes in good to excellent condition.
  • Boxes must be free of major damage, stains, liquid exposure, mold, smoke odors, and defacing (marker, pen, stickers, etc.).
  • Slight wear (minor dents or small scratches) may be accepted at a reduced rate.

3. Damage Policy

  • Boxes that are ripped, crushed, or have broken seals will not be accepted.
  • WBTSOK may offer partial payment (up to 50%) for minor damage at our discretion.
  • Any product deemed unsafe (broken seal, used, expired, opened) will be discarded and no payment will be issued.

4. Label Removal

  • Do not remove or tamper with any prescription labels. WBTSOK will professionally and safely remove all prescription information to protect your privacy.
  • Do not attempt to peel, cut, or damage the box to remove labels. Damaged packaging due to label removal will result in a reduction or denial of payment.

5. Expiration Dates

  • We only accept items with sufficient shelf life remaining. Check our Sell Page for the minimum expiration dates required.
  • Boxes with altered or covered expiration dates will be discarded.

6. Specific Brand Guidelines

Dexcom, OmniPod, Tandem, Medtronic, and other CGM or insulin pod products must:

  • Be in original packaging.
  • Contain sealed pods/sensors.
  • Match lot numbers and quantities as printed on the packaging.

7. Government-Issued Items

  • We do not accept test strips or supplies paid for by Medicare or Medicaid.
  • CGM products marked as "DME Beneficiaries" may be eligible.

8. International Products

We do not accept products labeled for international sale.

9. Product Listing

WBTSOK only purchases items listed on our website's sell page. Items not listed will not be purchased.

10. Shipping

  • WBTSOK provides a free prepaid USPS or FedEx shipping label.
  • If you use your own carrier, we will reimburse you up to $10 per shipment with receipt.

11. Return Policy for Unapproved Items

  • If your items do not meet our terms, we will notify you via email.
  • If you want unaccepted items returned, you must provide a prepaid, trackable return label (USPS preferred). Send via email to: webuydiabeticteststripsOK@gmail.com

12. Payment

  • Payment will be issued within 24 hours of processing your package.
  • We offer payment via PayPal, Zelle, Venmo, or mailed check.
  • Packages received on weekends or holidays may be processed the following business day.
  • WBTSOK is not responsible for delays caused by third-party payment processors.

13. Checks by Mail

  • Mailed checks may take up to 7 business days to arrive.
  • A check is considered lost after 15 calendar days. We will void and reissue the check upon request.

14. Dispute Window

You may dispute your payment amount within 15 days of issuance.

15. Wholesale Orders

  • Orders exceeding $1,000 may be subject to wholesale pricing.
  • WBTSOK reserves the right to refuse or renegotiate any wholesale order over $2,500.
  • Return shipping for rejected wholesale products will be paid by WBTSOK.

16. Order Validity

  • Payouts are based on the rates at the time your order is submitted, not when your package is received.
  • We recommend shipping your order within 15 days of submission.
